About Me

I enjoy complex puzzles and spend a lot of time studying obscure file formats.

I am semi-fluent in Japanese. I can read, write, and communicate conversationally without much assistance.

I would like to learn other languages as well, Hebrew, Greek, French, Portuguese, and possibly Tagalog, Hiligaynon, Russian, and Bulgarian. My favorite languages are languages that use non-latin scripts.

Programming Languages that I am most familiar with are C, C++, and Python. I am also familiar with HTML, PHP, MySQL, XML, Javascript, Lua, CSS, C#, Java, and Assembly (x86, x86_64, and ARM)
